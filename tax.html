Income Tax — Quick Guide (India sample)

This page provides a simple overview. Tax laws change — use an official calculator for precise filing.

Old vs New Regime

Two regimes exist: the old regime allows many deductions; the new regime offers lower slabs but fewer deductions.

Common Deductions

  • 80C (Investments such as PPF, ELSS, PF) — up to ₹1.5 lakh
  • 80D (Health insurance premium)
  • HRA (House Rent Allowance) — subject to conditions
